Family Promoting Stories and Lessons

Family traditions, stories, and teachings were onced passed down from parent to child and then to their childrens children. The unravelling of society has largely disrupted these chains among which are family recipes, heirlooms, and valuing our elders hard-earned wisdom.

Dr. Goose wants to provide an avenue for parents to reforge family bonds! If you have a family story, legend, or lesson, you can share the premise with Dr. Goose and he will illustrate and write a book with a credit to you and your family!
