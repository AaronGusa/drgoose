About Dr. Goose


Born in Saskatoon Saskatchewan, raised in Lacombe Alberta, Canada
he migrated south and now lives in the U.S.A.

Dr. Goose is a devoted storyteller with a passion fo sharing timeless principles that inspire and uplift. Through engaging narratives and enchanting illustrations, Dr. Goose aims to nurture the seeds of faith and wonder in every child's heart.

When not weaving magical tales, Dr. Goose enjoys exploring nature with his family, finding joy in life's simple moments.

He grew up in rural Saskatchewan and Alberta in Canada. As a youth he was a volunteer for town events, participated in highway cleanups, achieved a Queen Scout's Award, and worked as a support worker for disabled children.

As a father of 4, Dr. Goose has devoted his life to being a good father, teaching his children about life and the world through stories and fables. Dr. Goose's children have encouraged him to bring these stories to others.

Now Dr. Goose is making his stories available to you and your family!
