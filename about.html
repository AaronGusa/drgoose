About Dr. Goose


Born in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan, Canada this Canadian goose has migrated south!

Dr. Goose is a devoted storyteller with a passion fo sharing timeless principles that inspire and uplift. Through engaging narratives and enchanting illustrations, Dr. Goose aims to nurture the seeds of faith and wonder in every child's heart.

When not weaving magical tales, Dr. Goose enjoys exploring nature with his family finding joy in life's simple moments.

Dr. Goose grew up in rural Saskatchewan and Alberta in Canada. As a youth he volunteered for town events such as facepainting, participated in highway cleanups, achieved a Queen Scout's Award through Scouts Canada, and worked as a support worker for disabled children.


As a father of 4, Dr. Goose has devoted his life to being a good father. Teaching his children about life and the world has been through story-telling and fables. Dr. Goose children have encouraged him to bring the stories to others.

Now Dr. Goose is making his stories available to you!
